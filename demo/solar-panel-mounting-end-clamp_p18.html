Description of Solar Panel End Clamps

The solar panel end clamps are used to attach both framed and frameless PV modules to solar mounting rails, they go at the beginnings and ends of rows. The end clamps for solar panels are made in exact height to fit the thickness of PV requested PV modules. We have adjustable end clamps to meet different thickness of solar panels too.

Benefits of Solar Panel End Clamps

1.High-grade aluminum 6005-T5

2.Stainless steel bolt with hex head

3.Easy to fit, cost-efficient

4.Allow for easy adjustment on rails

5.Using appropriate hardware supplied by rail manufacturers, the clamps will fit nearly all types of rails.
