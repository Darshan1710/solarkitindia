Description of Flat Roof Solar Mounting Kits

The flat roof solar mounting kits are applicable to large scale flat roof solar panel installation. It allows solar array to tilt angle 5-30 degrees with the rooftop. The Solar Mounts for Flat Roofs can be pre-designed a fixed angle or use adjustable brackets for customer’s requests. They can be mounted directly on the concrete rooftop or mounted on blocks. The extruded aluminum olar mounting kits are highly pre-assembled to make the whole installation easy and quick to save labor time and cost.

2. Solar panel direction (horizontal or portrait)

3. Max wind load? And snow load?

4. Solar panel layout (rows*lines)
