Description of Z Brackets for Solar Panels

The Z brackets for solar panels are ideal for installation on motorhome roofs and other flat surface. The Solar Panel Z Brackets provided complete with all fasteners to secure the system easy and quick to the installation surface. Using Z brackets mount the solar panels will ensure the panels are safe and securely in the mounting place. The Solar Panel Z Brackets also leave a space between the solar panel and mounting space for airflow to cool the PV modules and prevent overheating.
