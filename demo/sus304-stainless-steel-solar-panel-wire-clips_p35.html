Description of Solar Panel Wire Clips

Proper cable support means securing cabling along solar panels and racking equipment, so the wire management is one of most important part in solar panel mounting system. The Stainless Steel Solar Wire Clips is the appropriate components to meet wire management requests. They are available in many forms to meet a variety of needs of solar panel installations. The solar panel wire clips are designed to fasten to module frames, normally, they can clamp 2 pieces of 4 pieces cables in diameter up to 4mm². With smart designs, the clips are easy to use and requires without any tools when install them. For most of items, we have stocks, we accept small orders for them and can delivery goods quickly. The Solar Wire Clips we supply are made from 304 stainless steel, but we are capable to supplying products to customer’s specifications such as in materials 201, 316 or carbon steel and so on. If you have a special requirement for our products, you can forward it to us, we will give you a proposal and offer samples for testing if requested.
